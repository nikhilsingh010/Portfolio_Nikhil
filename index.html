Hello, I am a digital product designer who creates meaningful user experiences to tackle real-world problems. I strive for simplicity, utility and consistency to advocate for user empowerment through:

1

Distilling user research into product requirements that are coherent with the business goals

2

Creating low-fidelity wireframes and information architecture to conceptualize the product

3

A systems-based approach for user interface design to keep a consistent visual vocabulary in the product

4

Rapid prototyping to conduct usability (or A/B) testing to iterate and evolve the product further.
